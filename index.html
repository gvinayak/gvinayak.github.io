About Me

I am a Researcher in the AI research group (AIRG) at the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory. I work on designing ML models for temporal sequences. Recently, I have been exploring large language models (LLMs) for modeling time-sensitive healthcare data. In the past, I have also delved into deep generative models and graph-based models for sequential data mining and recommendation systems.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science at the University of Washington where I worked with Tim Althoff. Even before, I was a Research Scientist at IBM Research, and completed my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi where I worked with Srikanta Bedathur and Abir De. Even before, I received my Bachelor's in CS from the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Jabalpur. I have also worked as an Applied Scientist-Intern at Amazon, and as an R&D-Intern at Siemens.
