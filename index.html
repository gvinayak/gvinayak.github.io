About Me

I am a Researcher in the AI research group (AIRG) at the Lawrence Livermore National Lab. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Scholar at the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science, University of Washington, where I worked with Tim Althoff, an AI Scientist at IBM Research, and also held positions as an Applied Scientist Intern at Amazon and an R&D Intern at Siemens.

My research advances methods for modeling medical and temporal data, combining techniques from machine learning and LLMs to drive progress in genomics, drug discovery, and personalized recommendations. My work has been featured in multiple media outlets, including IBM blogs, and recognized as "One of the greatest revelations of 2024". I was IIT Delhi's nominee for the ACM SIGKDD and ACM Doctoral Dissertation Awards, featured on IndiaAI by the Ministry of IT, Government of India, was a runner-up in NASSCOM's AI Gamechangers of India, and received the Outstanding Doctoral Paper Award at the conference on AI-ML Systems.
